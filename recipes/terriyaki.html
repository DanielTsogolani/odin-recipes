A quick and easy one-pan, kid-friendly skillet

Try this quick to make Terriyaki Beef skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 3/4 cup thick terriyaki sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped water chestnuts, drained
  • 2 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1 pound sausage
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sliced green onion

Directions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in
    in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes
  2. Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt and cumin. Pour
    tomato sauce over the beef, stir to coat and simmer until thickened, slightly
    about 5 minutes
